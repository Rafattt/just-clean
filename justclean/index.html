Our Services:

Regular Home Cleaning Checklist

Bedroom, Living Room & Common Areas:
-Dust all accessible surfaces
-Wipe down all mirrors and glass fixtures
-Wipe door handles and light switches
-Make beds with existing linen (washing linens will incur an extra fee)
-Clean all floor surfaces
-Pick up clothes off floor, fold and place on a chair
-Vacuum and mop floors
-Take out garbage and recycling
-Clean dust window sills
-Remove cobweb
-Empty and clean ashtrays
Bathrooms:
-Wash and sanitize the toilet, shower, bathtub and sink
-Dust all accessible surfaces
-Wipe down all mirrors and glass fixtures
-Wipe door handles and light switches
-Wipe down all tile walls
-Vacuum, or sweep and mop floor surfaces
-Take out garbage and change bag
-Clean the outside of cabinets
-Shake out bath mats
-Wipe down counter tops
-Fold towels
Kitchen:
-Wash and scrub sink
-Wash cabinet faces and appliances
-Clean inside and outside of microwave
-Dust and wipe clean countertops surfaces including tiles or backsplash
-Wipe door handles and light switches
-Wipe mirrors and glass fixtures
-Wipe down stove, outside oven, dishwasher and outside fridge
-Vacuum, clean and mop all floor surfaces
-Take out garbage and recycling
-Extras (upon request)
-Dust all window sills
-Wipe down the table and chairs

For a deeper clean, consider adding one or more cleaning extras. All extras add on an additional hour of time and cost:
-Inside cabinets
-Inside fridge
-Inside oven

Green Cleaning

Most of the cleaning products are made up of harsh cocktails of chemicals, which can be bad for your health, your family and your pets. Choosing green cleaning you choose Healthier Home, Purer Environment and It improves your indoor air quality. We care about your health and environment. We only use non-toxic, biodegradable cleaning products which are safe for your health, your family and pets.
